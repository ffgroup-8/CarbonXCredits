🗂️ Carbon X Credits — Master Knowledge Base
Compiled from: carbonxcredits.com and the Sanity Migration / Company Websites proposal (Forefathers Group, sent Jun 30, 2026). Update as brand guidelines and client docs are received.
Company Overview
  • Full Name: Carbon X Credits
  • What they do: Identify and permanently seal methane-leaking oil & gas wells across the U.S., converting the emissions reductions into verified carbon credits
  • HQ: Tulsa, Oklahoma
  • Website: carbonxcredits.com
  • Email: info@carbonxcredits.com
  • Client contacts: W. Landress (wlandress@carbonxcredits.com), T. Landress (tlandress@carbonxcredits.com)
  • Positioning line: "Turns legacy oil and gas pollution into climate-positive solutions"
Core Process / Services
  • Identify: Screen wells using production data and detection technology
  • Measure: Quantify methane emissions using EPA Method 21 standards
  • Plug: Execute well closure exceeding state standards
  • Monetize: Convert verified emissions reductions into carbon credits
Family of Companies
  • Carbon X Credits: Parent brand & carbon credit platform (main website)
  • ORR Energy: Well plugging & energy services (dedicated site, Phase 2)
  • True Methane Technologies: Methane measurement & detection technology (dedicated site, Phase 2)
  • Eagle Environmental Group: Environmental services (dedicated site, Phase 2)
Target Audience
  • Tech & data center companies with energy-intensive operations seeking offsets
  • Financial institutions investing in verified carbon credits
  • Energy sector stakeholders and operators
  • Environmental & sustainability-focused enterprises
Key Differentiators
  • Vertically integrated: identification, measurement, plugging, and monetization handled across the family of companies
  • Verified, standards-based credits (EPA Method 21; closures exceeding state standards)
  • Turns existing liabilities (abandoned wells) into measurable, sellable climate impact
Platform Notes (Sanity Migration)
  • Migrating from Webflow to Sanity CMS as one centralized content platform for the whole family of companies
  • Shared design system + reusable modules so new companies/acquisitions can be added quickly
  • Agentic content architecture: structured content built for both human visitors and AI-powered search
Tracked so far: 9 pages (6 Carbon X + 3 company homepages)  ·  54 status items across 6 categories  ·  company sitemaps expand in Phase 2
How This Tracker Works:

1. Phases: Work is delivered in 3 milestone phases (see Project Scope & Milestones). The phase pill shows where we are today; Phase 1 (Platform Foundation & Design) is currently in progress.

2. Status updates: When a page category is completed, tell Claude which page and which category (Design, Dev, SEO, Content, Sanity, Created) to mark as complete. Claude updates the pill from ○ Not Started → ◐ In Progress → ✓ Completed and recalculates the progress bars.

3. Git workflow: After each update, the HTML is saved and pushed to the Git repo so the client always sees current status.

4. Company websites: ORR Energy, True Methane Technologies, and Eagle Environmental Group are tracked in the Company Websites section. Each currently tracks a homepage placeholder; full sitemaps are added at the start of Phase 2.

5. Articles: Individual news/article pages are not tracked here; they're managed through Sanity. The /articles listing page itself IS tracked.

6. Knowledge base: Section 4 (Master Knowledge Base) serves as the single source of truth for all content creation across the site. Update as new information becomes available.

7. Content section structure: Each page in Section 3 follows this box hierarchy — Green SEO/AEO box: metadata (title, description, keywords, schema, AEO targets). Blue Content Created box: all 📌 Module copy blocks live here once content is written and approved. Purpose sits above both boxes as plain text. Never put module content outside the blue box.
